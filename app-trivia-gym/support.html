What is Trivia Gym?

Trivia Gym is a solo practice app for building trivia recall. It is not a live quiz show, multiplayer game, or ad-supported trivia contest. You train with curated questions using Self-Test or Voice practice modes.

Getting started

  1. Open Trivia Gym — the free starter pack loads on your device.
  2. In Settings, choose Self-Test or Voice Answer.
  3. On Home, start Quick Practice (10 / 30 / 50 / unlimited).
  4. Review past correct and incorrect questions from Home.

Voice mode permissions

Voice practice needs Microphone and Speech Recognition access. If you previously declined, go to iOS Settings → Trivia Gym and enable them, or use Self-Test mode instead.

Purchases & packs

The free starter includes curated questions, both study modes, and no ads. Additional category question packs are coming soon as optional one-time purchases.
