Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026 · Last updated: July 11, 2026

Trivia Gym (“the App”) is a solo trivia practice application published by RWND. This policy explains what information the App uses and what it does not.

Summary

  • No account is required.
  • We do not run ads.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • Practice progress stays on your device unless you choose otherwise in the future.

Information the App uses

On-device practice data

The App stores practice history, preferences (such as study mode and session length), and related stats locally on your device so you can review past questions and continue practice. This data is not uploaded to RWND servers as part of the current version.

Microphone and speech recognition (Voice mode)

If you enable Voice Answer mode, the App uses the microphone and Apple’s speech recognition APIs to convert what you say into text and grade your answer. Audio is processed to provide the Voice mode feature. The App does not use voice input for advertising.

You can deny microphone or speech permission and still use Self-Test mode. You can change permissions later in iOS Settings → Trivia Gym.

Purchases (future)

Optional question packs may be offered later as one-time In-App Purchases processed by Apple. Apple handles payment information under Apple’s privacy policy. RWND does not receive your full payment card details.

Information we do not collect (current version)

  • No account email or profile
  • No third-party advertising identifiers for tracking across apps
  • No analytics SDKs that send identifiable profiles to RWND in the current build

Data retention and deletion

Local practice data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear app data via iOS. Uninstalling Trivia Gym removes locally stored practice data for that installation.

Children

The App is intended as a general trivia practice tool and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. It does not require an account.

Changes

We may update this policy when features change (for example, if cloud sync or analytics are added). The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do.

Contact

Questions about privacy: open an issue at github.com/rwnd/apps/issues with the subject “Trivia Gym Privacy”.
